Overview

A comprehensive mobile and web solution that transforms how luxury resort guests discover, book, and maximize their stays. The app combines real-time availability, personalized memberships, and seamless payment processing into a unified platform. Our design prioritized intuitive navigation, trust-building UI patterns, and conversion optimization across mobile and desktop experiences.

The Problem

Resort guests faced fragmented booking experiences across multiple platforms. Existing solutions lacked integration between availability, membership benefits, and payment systems, creating friction during the booking process. Secondary guests couldn't track benefits, and staff couldn't access comprehensive reservation data.

Key Challenges

  • Guests unable to view membership benefits during booking
  • No unified platform across mobile and web
  • Payment processing delays and abandoned carts
  • Limited visibility into available dates and room types
  • Secondary guests had no app access or account features

The Solution

We designed an integrated platform that unifies booking, membership, and payment. The interface uses progressive disclosure to guide users from exploration through checkout without overwhelming them. Real-time availability, personalized recommendations, and instant membership benefits integration accelerate decision-making and reduce abandonment.

Core Features

  • Real-time room availability with visual calendar
  • Integrated membership tier system with instant benefits
  • One-tap payment processing with saved methods
  • Secondary guest account access and tracking
  • Push notifications for personalized offers
  • Admin dashboard for staff operations

Design Process

Research & Discovery

We conducted interviews with 20+ frequent resort guests to understand pain points in current booking flows. Analytics from the legacy system revealed 45% cart abandonment rates, primarily during payment steps. This research directly informed our streamlined checkout design.

Wireframing & Flows

We mapped user journeys for three personas: luxury travelers, loyalty members, and travel groups. Wireframes prioritized visible benefits and social proof throughout the booking experience. We tested three different membership presentation strategies with users.

Design System & Iteration

Built a comprehensive design system with mobile-first components. Conducted 4 rounds of user testing, iterating on payment flow, calendar interaction, and membership display. Final designs achieved 89% task success rate in usability studies.
